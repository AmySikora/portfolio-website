My Journey into Web Development

I grew up in Phoenix, Arizona, a city named after the mythological bird that dies in an inferno, is reborn, and rises from its ashes stronger than before.

My logo reflects my career change journey from higher ed to tech by embracing change, chasing something new, and rising into web development with fresh energy and purpose.

Before Web Development

Before diving into tech, I worked in higher education helping students succeed. I led service initiatives, built award-winning systems, and loved finding ways to turn slow, paper-based processes into streamlined, automated workflows.

I taught myself tools like SQL, Banner and Laserfiche, built custom e-forms, and automated just about everything I could. Eventually I enrolled in a full-stack web development program to go deeper and build more.

Now I bring curiosity, strategy, and a people-first mindset to apps that are functional, friendly, and a little more fun.
