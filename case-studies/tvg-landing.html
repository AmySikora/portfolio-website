Overview

I built a responsive marketing page to explain how Ticket VeriGuard keeps listings in sync so the same seat cannot sell twice. The page focuses on clear messaging, trust, and a direct path into the live demo.

The Problem

When sellers list the same seats on multiple marketplaces, duplicate sales lead to chargebacks, angry fans at the gate, and damaged marketplace trust. The concept needs to be communicated simply before diving into integration details.

My Role

Product concept, branding, UX writing, front-end development, deployment.

Highlights

  • Explains the conflict problem for buyers, sellers, and marketplaces.
  • Uses a consistent, professional visual identity for the Ticket VeriGuard brand.
  • Connects to the technical prototype and future partner conversations.

Results

This project shows my ability to define a real-world problem, design a solution narrative, and present it in a way that speaks to both technical stakeholders and business partners.
