Ticket VeriGuard — Landing Page

Brand identity and product pitch for preventing duplicate ticket listings across marketplaces.

View Site View Code

Overview

I built a responsive marketing page to explain how Ticket VeriGuard keeps listings in sync so the same seat cannot sell twice. The page focuses on clear messaging, trust, and a direct path into the live demo.

The Problem

When sellers list the same seats on multiple marketplaces, duplicate sales lead to chargebacks, angry fans at the gate, and damaged marketplace trust. The concept needs to be communicated simply before diving into integration details.

My Role

Product concept, branding, UX writing, front-end development, deployment.

Highlights

  • Explains the conflict problem for buyers, sellers, and marketplaces in clear language.
  • Establishes a consistent, professional visual identity for the Ticket VeriGuard brand.
  • Connects directly to the technical prototype for deeper partner conversations.

Results

This project shows my ability to define a real-world problem, design a solution narrative, and present it in a way that speaks to both technical stakeholders and business partners — a foundation for pitching Ticket VeriGuard to real marketplaces.
